# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: March 12, 2026*

Lectifio does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app operates entirely on your device.

- No account or sign-in is required.
- No analytics or tracking are used.
- No data is transmitted to any server.

Books, highlights, notes, and reading progress are stored locally on your device and, if you have iCloud enabled, synced via your personal iCloud account. This data is not accessible to us.

If you have any questions, contact us at [sendmails91@gmail.com](mailto:sendmails91@gmail.com).
